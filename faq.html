FAQs

How do I search for a pet?
Visit our homepage to search your location, pet age and pet type (cat or dog) and receive results specified to your desires!

How often is FindingFido updated?
We update our site results EVERYDAY to deliver the most accurate results in your search for a new companion.

How can I meet a pet I find?
Follow the link to its details page to learn more about the pet and its rescue organization. Some shelters have different adoption approaches and it's important to know how their systems operate. We are supported by PetFinder in which you can learn more about the animals and their rescues.

How Long Will It Take to Hear Back from the Adoption Group?
The response rate depends on the organization's competnece. We, personally, cannot garuntee a pet or rescue response. We suggest contacting the group outside of PetFinder as well.

What are the adoption rates?
Petfinder doesn’t dictate adoption group policy, including adoption requirements and fees. If you’re interested in a specific pet, please contact the adoption group that created the pet listing through the Pet Profile page or Member Home Page.

Why are there adoption fees?
Adoption "donations" are spent towards housing, feeding and sheltering rescued animals as well as any medical care that their pets may receive.

Why wasn't I selected to adopt?
Thank you for thinking adoption first! Each shelter and rescue group has its own adoption process. We do not dictate adoption procedures to our Petfinder members, although we do encourage them to do everything possible to facilitate finding good homes for their adoptable pets. We hope you won’t let this one experience change your mind about providing a loving home to a pet in need. There are many animal shelters and rescue groups with many more adoptable pets waiting for their forever homes! Thank you for helping an adoptable pet and we wish you the best of luck in your search to find a new member of your family. If you have specific questions or concerns about a shelter, please contact us below!
